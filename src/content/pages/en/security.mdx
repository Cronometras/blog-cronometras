*   **Automatic Expiration:** Trusted devices automatically expire after 30 days.
*   **Manual Revocation:** Remove trusted devices at any time from the security panel.
*   **Complete List:** View all devices that have accessed your account.

*   **2 Session Limit:** Maximum of 2 simultaneous active sessions: one on PC/web and one on mobile/tablet.
*   **One Session per Platform:** When logging in on a platform, the previous session on that same platform is automatically closed.
*   **Real-Time View:** List of active sessions with device, location, and last activity.
*   **Remote Logout:** End any active session from the security panel.
