## 2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website, when visited by a user, stores on the user's device (computer, tablet, smartphone, etc.) through the browser. Cookies allow the Website to recognise the user's browser and store certain information, such as the user's preferences, language, the device from which they access, or usage statistics.

## 3. Types of Cookies Used by this Website

The types of cookies used by this Website are detailed below:

### 3.1. Technical cookies (strictly necessary)

These allow the user to browse the Website and access its various functionalities. Without these cookies, the basic browsing services cannot be provided. The Website cannot function properly without them, so **they do not require the user's consent** under Article 22.2 of the Spanish LSSI-CE.

Among these cookies are, for example, those that keep the user's session open, remember language preferences, or balance load between servers.

### 3.2. Preference or personalisation cookies

These allow the user to access the service with certain pre-defined general features based on a series of criteria on the user's device, such as the language, browser type, or region from which the service is accessed. If the user does not accept these cookies, the service may not work properly.

### 3.3. Analytics or measurement cookies

These allow the data controller of the Website to track and analyse the behaviour of users on the Website, including the quantification of advertising impacts. The information collected is used to measure the activity of the Website in order to introduce improvements based on the analysis of the usage data of the service by users.

This Website uses **Google Analytics 4**, an analytics service provided by Google Ireland Limited. The information generated by Google Analytics cookies about your use of the Website (including your IP address, which will be anonymised before storage) will be directly transmitted and stored by Google on its servers. Google will use this information on behalf of the holder for the purpose of evaluating your use of the Website, compiling reports on the activity of the Website, and providing other services related to the activity of the Website and the use of the Internet. You can obtain more information about how Google processes your privacy at [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

Analytics cookies **are only installed if the user has previously accepted them** through the Website's cookie banner.

### 3.4. Marketing or behavioural advertising cookies

These allow the management of the advertising spaces included on the Website in the most effective way possible. These cookies store information on user behaviour obtained through continuous observation of their browsing habits, which allows a specific profile to be developed to display advertising based on that profile.

Currently, **the Website does not use marketing or behavioural advertising cookies**.

## 5. Legal Basis

The legal basis for the processing of data obtained through technical cookies (Section 3.1) is the execution of the legal relationship or the provision of the service requested by the user, and they do not require the user's consent.

The legal basis for the processing of data obtained through the rest of the cookies (preferences, analytics and marketing) is **the user's express consent**, in accordance with Article 6.1.a of the GDPR. The user may grant, deny or revoke their consent at any time, without this affecting the lawfulness of the prior processing based on consent.

## 6. Retention Period

Cookies will be retained on the user's device for the time necessary for the purpose for which they were installed, and for a maximum of the following periods:

- **Session technical cookies:** deleted when the browser is closed.
- **Persistent technical cookies (preferences):** up to 1 year.
- **Analytics cookies (Google Analytics 4):** up to 14 months (Google's default configuration).
- **Marketing cookies:** the Website does not currently use marketing cookies.
