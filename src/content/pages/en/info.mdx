    content: "Creating a study is the starting point in CRONOMETRAS. To ensure accurate analysis, the following mandatory fields are required:",

*   **Study Name (name):** Unique identifier for your study.
*   **Company (company):** Name of the company to which the study belongs.
*   **Date (date):** Date the study was conducted.
*   **Activity Scale:**
    *   **Normal:** 100 (Default value). Represents the standard work pace.
    *   **Optimal:** 133 (Default value). Represents the optimal work pace, achievable under ideal conditions.

<Section
  section={{
    title: "Optional Fields: Additional Detail",
    content:
      "Enhance your studies with additional information using the following optional fields:",
  }}
/>

*   **Study Number (study_number):** Internal numeric identifier.
*   **Operator (operator):** Person performing the studied task.
*   **Section (section):** Area or department where the task is performed.
*   **Reference (reference):** Reference number or identification code.
*   **Machine (machine):** Machine used in the task.
*   **Tools (tools):** Tools employed.
*   **Technician (technician):** Person conducting the study.
